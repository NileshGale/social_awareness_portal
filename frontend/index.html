Digital Protection

Cyber Safety Awareness

The internet can be a dangerous place. Protect yourself and your family from online threats, cyberbullying, and digital fraud.

Online Privacy

Protect your personal data, use strong passwords, and be careful about what you share online. Your digital footprint matters.

Cyberbullying Prevention

Recognize cyberbullying, know how to report it, and support victims. Online harassment is a crime under the IT Act.

Financial Fraud

Protect yourself from phishing, UPI fraud, and online scams. Never share OTPs, card details or passwords with anyone.

Cyber Crime Helpline

Report cyber crimes immediately at 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline) or at cybercrime.gov.in. Early reporting increases recovery chances.
