Cyber Safety
Stop UPI Fraud — Never Share Your OTP
India reported over 50,000 cyber fraud cases in 2024. Fraudsters pose as bank officials, government officers, or lottery agents to steal your money. REMEMBER: No bank, government office, or UPI app will ever ask for your OTP, PIN, or password. Report cyber fraud at 1930 immediately.

Mental Health
It is Okay to Not Be Okay — Seek Help
India has a mental health crisis — over 150 million people need care, but only 3 in 10 ever seek help. The stigma around mental illness is literally costing lives. Talking about your struggles is a sign of strength, not weakness. If you or someone you know is struggling, call iCall: 9152987821. You are not alone.

Social Evils
Dowry is a Crime — Stand Against It
Every 90 minutes, a woman in India dies due to dowry-related violence. The Dowry Prohibition Act 1961 makes giving and taking dowry a criminal offense with up to 5 years imprisonment. If you are facing dowry demands or harassment, you are not alone. Contact the One Stop Centre at 181 or file an FIR under IPC 304B.

Environment
Plastic-Free Nagpur — Our Responsibility
Nagpur generates over 1,100 tons of waste daily — much of it plastic that chokes our rivers, lakes, and soil. A plastic-free Nagpur is possible if each one of us acts today. Carry cloth bags, refuse single-use plastic, and segregate your waste. Every small step adds up to a cleaner, healthier city for our children.

Education
Education is Every Child's Right — No Child Left Behind
The Right to Education Act guarantees free and compulsory education to every child aged 6–14 in India. Yet millions of children — especially girls and children from marginalized communities — are denied this right every day. If you know a child who is not in school, report to the District Education Officer or call 1098. Every child deserves to learn.
